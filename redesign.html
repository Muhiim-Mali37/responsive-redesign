Our Story

Hanto Restaurant offers delicious dining, takeout and delivery to Ashland, MA. It is a cornerstone in the Ashland community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff. Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
